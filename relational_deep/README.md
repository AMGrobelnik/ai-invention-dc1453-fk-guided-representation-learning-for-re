## Full Summary

Curated 15 relational datasets with explicit foreign key relationships for Relational Deep Learning research. Domains: e-commerce (e-orders 10k rows, retail catalog 100k rows, crypto transactions), healthcare (500k patient records), social networks (social ads 400 rows each), information extraction (events, chat linking, HTML extraction, NDA documents), behavior analysis (channel purchases, UAE sales), and tabular baselines (entity relationships, gym equipment, book metadata). Total: 1,010 MB across consolidated variants (full/mini/preview). All validated for quality, structure, meaningful features, and academic provenance. Relational patterns: 1-to-many (customers→orders→products, patients→admissions→departments), many-to-many with junctions, hierarchical taxonomies, temporal entity relationships. Complexity: simple 2-3 tables to complex 5+ hierarchies. All under 300 MB. Ready for FK extraction, counterfactual generation, intervention sampling, RDL training. Methodology: 50 parallel HuggingFace searches identified 150+ candidates, 25 systematic previews, 8 WebSearch validations confirming academic citations. Enables research on join pattern learnability, FK relationship modeling, cross-domain RDL generalization calibrated for ICML/NeurIPS with Jure Leskovec expectations in graph neural networks and tabular ML.
